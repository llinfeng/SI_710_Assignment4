Cost of chatting/negotiation

We may well leave the chatting channel "free" for users in the experiment, and apply different accounting scheme in the analysis phase. To be more precise, we may then measure the cost in two ways: 1) accounting for all conversations, line by line; or 2) truncate all the "effective" negotiations before the final deal is sealed.

==> Solution: to end the chat right after a deal is sealed by two parties. Then, the time will serve as a good proxy of cost.
